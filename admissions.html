Are you interested in attending the Ohio State University?

What tests do I need to take to apply?

All applicants are required to have completed the ACT or SAT for first-year applicants to the Columbus, Ohio location. These scores must come directly from the testing agencies. For applicants who have been out of high school for more than two years, you are not required to submit scores by your term of enrollment.

What is the deadline to apply?

Early action applicants are required to submit all application materials by November 1st, 2024. All regular decision applicants must submit their application materials by January 15th, 2025.

What materials are required for my application?

You will need to submit your official transcripts, official SAT or ACT scores, and an optional recommendation letter from a teacher or counselor. Additionally, students whose first language is not English must also submit TOEFL, IELTS, or IELTS indicator scores.

How do I apply?

The Ohio State University accepts Common Application for admission to the Columbus, Ohio campus.
